Frequently Asked Questions
How do I upload my menu?
Go to Menu Upload, choose a PDF or text file, and click Upload. The AI will extract ingredients automatically using the RAG pipeline.
How does the prediction engine work?
The AI analyzes your historical order data and applies seasonality factors to predict 7-day ingredient demand. It uses Groq's LLM for natural language queries and PostgreSQL for data retrieval.
What questions can I ask the chatbot?
You can ask about stock levels, predictions, menu analysis, cost optimization, supplier recommendations, and ingredient substitutes. Examples:
• "What is the predicted need for Chicken and Rice?"
• "Which ingredients are low in stock?"
• "Suggest substitutes for Cream"
How does ingredient matching work?
The system uses PostgreSQL regex (word-boundary matching) to precisely match ingredients from your query to inventory items, preventing false matches like "Ice" matching "Basmati Rice".
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored in your local PostgreSQL database (accessible via pgAdmin). This includes menu documents, inventory, orders, and AI insights. Views like v_chatbot_insights are available for easy querying.
